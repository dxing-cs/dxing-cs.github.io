Biography

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University since Sept. 2019, advised by Prof. Gang Pan. I am also very honored to work with Prof. Qian Zheng. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University and my master's degree in Computer Science at Zhejiang University. Before my PhD studies, I worked at Alibaba as an algorithm engineer, led by Dr. Hongxia Yang. From Sept. 2022 to Sept. 2023, I am a joint PhD student at Nanyang Technological University, under the supervision of Prof. Bo An.

My recent research primarily focuses on deep reinforcement learning and its applications, particularly in the realm of robust multi-agent cooperation. I have devised solutions aimed at enhancing the diversity of teamwork, optimizing their functionality on resource-constrained platforms, and elucidating their causal interpretations. Additionally, I am also interested in both bio-based and bio-inspired computational methodologies.

I am currently on the 2023-2024 industrial/academic job market, looking for job opportunities in the field of reinforcement learning/machine learning and their applications. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested!
